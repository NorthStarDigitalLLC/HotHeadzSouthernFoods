Hot Headz Southern Foods started as a dream to bring real, homestyle cooking to DeRidder. Today, we're proud to serve breakfast and lunch made daily with love.

Our story

Hot Headz Southern Foods opened its doors in 2020 with a simple mission: serve fresh, filling, from-scratch meals that remind you of Sunday dinner at grandma's. What started as a small cafeteria‑style spot quickly became a local favorite.

We believe food should be honest – no shortcuts, no premade sysco boxes. Just real ingredients, cooked with care, and served with a smile.

Made fresh daily

Our kitchen starts before sunrise. From scratch‑made biscuits to our famous crawfish boils, everything is prepared in‑house. We don't just heat food – we cook it, and you can taste the difference.
