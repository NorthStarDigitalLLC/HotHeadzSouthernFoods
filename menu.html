Floating front card. The rest orbit behind it.

This version treats the categories like a real holographic stack. One category sits at the front, the rest curve behind it in a circular belt. Cycle left or right until the category you want comes forward, then click it to drill into that category’s items. When you click a category, the same carousel becomes the item carousel for that category.

Step 1
Cycle
Spin through the categories until the one you want reaches the front.
Step 2
Select
Click the front category to enter that menu: lunch, breakfast, drinks, dessert, and more.
Step 3
Items
The same floating carousel becomes the item view, with the chosen item sitting at the front.
Structure: categories come from menu-data.html, and once one is chosen, its items become the next carousel layer.
