Chef notes

The “special touch” recipe

Here is what elevates a restaurant site from “just a menu” to a premium digital experience:

1
Photography or strong dish visualsEven a few quality hero images drastically increase perceived value.
2
Live-feeling statusOpen now, countdowns, featured specials, and urgency make the site feel current.
3
Clear action hierarchyCall, order, and directions should always be obvious without overwhelming the screen.
4
Micro-interaction polishHover, tap, modal details, and subtle motion create the premium impression.
5
Consistent voiceUse warm, confident copy that sounds like the restaurant, not like generic software.
