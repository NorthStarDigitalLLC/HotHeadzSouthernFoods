Meet the Family

The folks behind every plate.

Hot Headz opened in 2020 — right when DeRidder needed a real plate to sit down to most. We're a family operation, top to bottom. Grandparents, parents, kids, grandkids — all of us here every week.

Every biscuit rolled out by hand. Every plate of red beans simmered low and slow. Every catfish dredged and fried to order. That's not marketing — that's just how Mama taught us.
