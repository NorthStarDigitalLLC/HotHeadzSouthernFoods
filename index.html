Southern Breakfast & Lunch in DeRidder, LA

Hot Headz Southern Foods is DeRidder’s home for made-from-scratch Southern breakfast and Cajun lunch plates. Every morning our kitchen fires up to make biscuits, gravy, and Southern breakfast plates. By lunchtime we’re serving red beans and rice, fried chicken, catfish plates, smothered pork chops, chicken & sausage gumbo, jambalaya, shrimp po-boys, meatloaf, and homemade desserts like bread pudding. The lunch plate rotates every day — check our Facebook each morning to see what’s on.

We’re a locally owned, family-operated restaurant that has been feeding DeRidder and the wider Beauregard Parish community since 2020. Whether you stop in to dine, grab a carry-out plate on the way to work, order bulk delivery for a job site or event, or tap the DoorDash button for hot food at your door, Hot Headz has you covered.

How to Order

Dine-in — walk into our DeRidder location at 2741 US-190 during business hours. Family-friendly and wheelchair accessible with free parking.

Carry-out — call ahead at (337) 221-1035 and your order will be ready when you arrive.

Local delivery — we deliver bulk orders of 10 or more plates within 10 miles of DeRidder. Perfect for job sites, offices, churches, and events. Learn more about delivery.

DoorDash — order Hot Headz on DoorDash for single-order delivery across the DeRidder area.
