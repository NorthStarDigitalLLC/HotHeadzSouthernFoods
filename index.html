Southern Restaurant in DeRidder, Louisiana

Hot Headz Southern Foods is DeRidder’s home for made-from-scratch Southern breakfast and Cajun lunch plates. Every morning our kitchen fires up to make biscuits, gravy, and Southern breakfast plates from scratch. By lunchtime we’re serving red beans and rice, fried chicken, catfish plates, smothered pork chops, chicken and sausage gumbo, jambalaya, shrimp po-boys, meatloaf, and homemade desserts like Louisiana bread pudding. The lunch plate rotates every day — check our daily plate banner each morning.

We’re a locally owned, family-operated restaurant that has been feeding DeRidder and the wider Beauregard Parish community since 2020. Whether you stop in to dine, grab a carry-out plate on the way to work, order bulk delivery for a job site or event, or tap the DoorDash button for hot food at your door, Hot Headz has you covered.
