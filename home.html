Our Story

Home cooking
with no shortcuts.

Hot Headz Southern Foods has been feeding DeRidder since 2020. Every plate is made the same morning it's served — no reheated shortcuts, no freezer surprises. Just real Southern cooking done the way grandma would approve of.

Walk in, grab a tray, pick your plate. Breakfast runs from opening until it's gone, lunch keeps coming until 2. On Thursdays through Saturdays, crawfish season means we stay open evenings too.
