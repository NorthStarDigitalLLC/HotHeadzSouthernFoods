FAQ

Quick Answers.

What is the minimum order for delivery?
10 plates within a 10-mile radius of our DeRidder location. For smaller orders, please use DoorDash or call ahead for carry-out.
How much do plates cost?
1 meat + 2 sides — $12.99/plate · 2 meats + 2 sides — $15.99/plate · 3 meats + 2 sides — $19.99/plate · Child plate (10 & under) — $6.99 · Extra side — $2.99 each. Use the plate builder above for an instant estimate.
How much does crawfish cost?
$5.99/lb, available Thu–Sun, 4–8 PM during season. Market price changes — call to confirm.
How much advance notice do you need?
For 10–30 plates we recommend 24 hours notice. For orders over 30 plates, give us 48 hours. Same-day orders are sometimes possible — call us to check.
Is there a delivery fee?
Delivery fees vary based on distance and order size. Call (337) 221-1035 for an exact quote.
What plates can I get?
Our daily Southern lunch plates — the menu rotates. Common staples include red beans & rice, fried chicken, catfish, smothered pork chops, gumbo, jambalaya, and meatloaf with classic sides. Today's menu loads automatically in the plate builder above.
Can you handle dietary restrictions?
We do our best. Tell us when you call and we'll work with you. Note that our kitchen handles common allergens.
Need 50+ plates?
Big crews, weddings, corporate events — we can handle it. Give us a couple days and we'll feed the whole crowd.
