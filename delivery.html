FAQ

Quick Answers.

What is the minimum order for delivery?
10 plates within a 10-mile radius of our DeRidder location. For smaller orders, please use DoorDash or call ahead for carry-out.
How much advance notice do you need?
For 10–30 plates we recommend 24 hours notice. For orders over 30 plates, give us 48 hours. Same-day orders are sometimes possible — call us to check.
Is there a delivery fee?
Delivery fees vary based on distance and order size. Call (337) 221-1035 for an exact quote when you place your order — no surprise charges.
What plates can I get delivered?
Our daily Southern lunch plates: red beans & rice, fried chicken, catfish, smothered pork chops, chicken & sausage gumbo, jambalaya, meatloaf, and rotating specials. Each plate comes with sides and cornbread. Check Facebook each morning for today’s offerings.
Can you handle dietary restrictions?
We do our best. Tell us when you call and we’ll work with you on vegetarian options, no-pork plates, or other needs. Note that our kitchen handles common allergens.
What about weekend delivery?
Yes — we deliver during business hours: Saturday 6 AM–2 PM and Sunday 7 AM–3 PM. Weekend orders may need extra notice.
Need 50+ plates?
Big crews, weddings, corporate events — we can handle it. Give us a couple days to plan and we’ll feed the whole crowd.
